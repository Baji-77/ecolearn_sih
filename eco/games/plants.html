How to Play

  • Drag seeds from the left panel to empty plots
  • Drag the watering can to water your plants
  • Plants grow through stages: seed → sprout → mature
  • Click on mature plants to harvest them
  • Earn points for each harvested plant
  • Use buttons to water or harvest all plants at once
  • Press restart to begin a new game
